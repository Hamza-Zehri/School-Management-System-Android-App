# Privacy Policy for School Manager

**Last Updated: April 20, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **School Manager** ("the App") handles your information. 

### 1. Information We Collect
The App is designed as a **100% Offline** school management tool. It collects and stores the following information locally on your device:
- **Student Information**: Name, Father's Name, Registration Number, Class, Section, Gender, Date of Birth, Admission Date, and Address.
- **Guardian Information**: Name and Phone Numbers (for SMS notifications).
- **Employee Information**: Name, Father's Name, Phone Number, CNIC, Salary details, and Attendance.
- **Academic Records**: Exam scores, marks, and attendance records.

### 2. No Data Transmission
**We do not collect or transmit any of your data to external servers.**
Because the app is entirely offline, all data entered into the app remains strictly on your local device's database (SQLite). We do not have access to your students' records, financial data, or employee information.

### 3. Permissions We Request
To provide its core functionality, the app requests the following permissions:
- **SMS Permission**: Required to send automated and manual SMS notifications to parents for student/employee absences or fee reminders.
- **Storage/File Access**: Required to import student data from Excel files and to export backups or reports to your device's memory.

### 4. Data Storage and Security
All data is stored in an encrypted/localized database on your Android device. You are responsible for maintaining the physical security of your device and managing your own backups using the integrated Backup & Restore feature.

### 5. Third-Party Services
The app uses standard Android system services (like the native SMS Manager) to perform actions. These services may have their own privacy policies. The app does not integrate with any analytics, tracking, or advertising networks.

### 6. Children's Privacy
The App is intended for use by school administrators. While it stores information about students (who may be children), this information is provided by the school administration and is never shared or transmitted anywhere outside the local device.

### 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
